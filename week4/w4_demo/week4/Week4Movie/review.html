A REVIEW of The 100

Samuel Morales, August 8, 2024

The 100 is a really great show that I encourage others to watch. It has 7 seasons which has plenty of binge worthy episodes for you to watch and the best thing is that it is not the type of show that starts off slow and then takes a while to start getting good, The 100 has your curiosity going right from the start. Watch The 100!

Trivia
  • In the books Wells is a main character, but in the show was killed of in season 1.
  • The 100's first episode generated 1.3 million viewers in 2014.
  • Raven was meant to be killed off in season 1, instead she became a show regular.
